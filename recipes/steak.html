Lasagna

Description

This Steak is so juicy!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat the pan and the olive oil until you see smoke coming out
  2. Sear the steak for 2mins. each side
  3. Add butter,rosemary, garlic, and thyme
  4. Baste butter and rest for 5mins.
