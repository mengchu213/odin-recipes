Carbonara

Description

This Carbonara is so creamy you'll like it!

Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolk
  • Guanciale
  • Regianno cheese
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dante
  2. Fry the Guanciale until fat is rendered
  3. Mix the egg and cheese
  4. Transfer the pasta to the egg and cheese mixture with the guanciale
